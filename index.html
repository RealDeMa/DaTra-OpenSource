Features

Simple by design

📡

No Internet Required

Everything happens locally over Bluetooth. Your files never leave your devices.

⚡

Both Directions

Send files from phone to laptop and from laptop to phone – fully bidirectional.

📁

Any File Type

Photos, documents, videos, archives – transfer whatever you need without restrictions.

🔒

No Account Needed

No login, no cloud, no tracking. Just pair your devices and go.

🪶

Lightweight

One Python file per platform. No heavy dependencies, no installers, no background services.

🖥️

Desktop Icon

Launch from your app drawer or taskbar like any other app – on both Linux and Windows.

Usage

How it works

01

Pair Devices

Pair your phone and laptop via Bluetooth once in your system settings.

02

Start App

Open DaTra on your laptop and choose Receive or Send.

03

Select File

Pick the file you want to transfer on whichever device is sending.

04

Done

The file transfers instantly. No confirmation steps, no cloud sync delay.

Get DaTra

Pick your platform and get started. No installation wizard, no account – just download and run.
